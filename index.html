Identify. Analyze. Report.

PrivaSee is an OSINT Analysis tool that simplifies the security assessment process for your business. We collect and assess the external threats, as well as perform brand analysis to provide intuitive insights and data visualizations for you.

Why PrivaSee?

$3,920,00
On average, it would cost $3.92 million for the businesses in the US for a data breach, which will devastate a business in terms of financial cost and company reputation.
47%
47% of C-Suites believe the main cause of a data breach is employee negligence. However, sometimes it can be hard to detect your employee's online presence.
PrivaSee is here to help you obtain a better overview of the public data related to your business, and provide insights for potential data breach and reputation threats.

Data Security Scanning

Collect information online through Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) to check for data risks due to employee negligence. Provide intuitive reports to conceptualize existing threats.

  • Phishing Assessment
  • Compromised Accounts Detections
  • Custom Input of Data

Employee Presence Analysis

Understand your employee’s online presence with business emails to prevent potential data leakages.

Company Branding Investigation

Obtain an overview of your business’ online presence for your next step of successful brand building.
